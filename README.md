## 📚 **About This Project**

**Vitra** is a design thinking project focused on understanding and addressing **employment challenges faced by transgender individuals**.

Instead of relying purely on assumptions or secondary data, this project is grounded in:

- 🧍 **Direct, in-person street interviews** with transgender individuals  
- 🏢 **Conversations with corporate stakeholders and recruiters**  
- 📊 Research into structural, social, and economic barriers  

The goal was not just to study the problem, but to **translate insight into a tangible solution**.

---

## 🧠 **Design Approach**

This project follows a **human-centered design thinking framework**:

| Stage | Focus |
|------|------|
| Empathize | Listening to lived experiences |
| Define | Identifying core employment barriers |
| Ideate | Exploring possible interventions |
| Prototype | Designing a functional solution |
| Test | Evaluating usability and relevance |

---

## 🧍 **Ground Research**

Real understanding came from **stepping outside the screen**.

### What we did:
- Conducted **in-person interviews** with transgender individuals in public spaces  
- Spoke to individuals across different age groups and backgrounds  
- Engaged with **corporate professionals** to understand hiring perspectives  

### What emerged:
- Employment barriers are not just about skills, but **access, perception, and safety**
- Many opportunities fail at the **entry point itself**
- Corporate intent and real inclusion often **don’t align in practice**

---

## 🔍 **Key Insights**

- 🧩 **Access Gap** → Jobs exist, but are not accessible or safe to apply for  
- ⚖️ **Bias & Filtering** → Hiring decisions influenced before interviews even begin  
- 🧠 **Confidence Erosion** → Repeated rejection impacts self-perception  
- 🔒 **Lack of Safe Platforms** → No trusted space for inclusive opportunities  

---

## 💡 **Solution – Vitra App Prototype**

A **job-access and support platform** designed specifically for transgender individuals.

### Core Features:

- 🔎 **Verified Inclusive Employers**  
  Curated opportunities from organizations committed to inclusivity  

- 🧾 **Safe Job Discovery**  
  Reducing risk and uncertainty in application processes  

- 🧠 **Skill & Resource Support**  
  Guidance for resumes, interviews, and career growth  

- 🤝 **Supportive Ecosystem**  
  Bridging the gap between job seekers and employers  

---

## 🧪 **Prototype Focus**

The prototype emphasizes:

- Simplicity and trust  
- Low barrier to entry  
- Emotional safety alongside functionality  

Because for many users:
> The challenge isn’t just finding a job.  
> It’s feeling safe enough to apply.
